Program Requirements and Sequences

Retention Requirements

Students must carefully follow all program requirements in order to remain in good academic standing.

  • Sequence in Audio and Music Production and Interdisciplinary Technologies: Students must meet and continue to maintain an overall cumulative GPA of 2.50.
  • Sequence in Game Design: Students must maintain an overall cumulative GPA of 2.50 and a cumulative GPA of 3.0 within the major. Standards for Progress in the Major

Students may continue in the Creative Technologies program if they maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 and demonstrate competency through an intermediate portfolio review. This review ensures that students are progressing appropriately in both technical and creative areas and helps prepare them for advanced coursework in the program.

Sequences in Creative Technologies

Accelerated Creative Technologies Sequence

High-achieving students with a cumulative GPA of 3.20 or higher may apply to the accelerated sequence one year before completing their undergraduate degree.

Audio and Music Production Sequence

This sequence focuses on sound recording, audio production, sound design, music composition, theory, and business practices.

Game Design Sequence

This sequence provides concentrated study in game design and production.

Interdisciplinary Technologies Sequence

This sequence emphasizes mobile, web, and computer programming concepts, UI/UX, motion graphics, and interactivity.
